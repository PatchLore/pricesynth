I built this tool after negotiating SaaS contracts and realizing list prices rarely reflect actual market rates.

This service aggregates publicly available pricing information from official sources including vendor websites, historical web archives (Archive.org), UK public sector contracts (Contracts Finder), and published partner documentation.

This is not insider information. It is competitive market data derived entirely from public signals, analyzed systematically.

All reports are generated using automated tools that collect only publicly accessible data. We do not scrape social media, review sites, or private communities.
