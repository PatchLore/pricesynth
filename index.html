About us

Built by a solo founder who was tired of overpaying for software.

I started Shadow Pricing Auditor after negotiating a SaaS contract and realizing that the "list price" wasn't the price most companies were actually ending up with.

Public discussions, archived pricing pages, and partner documentation often tell a very different story than marketing pages. But that information is scattered, outdated, and hard to synthesize.

So I built a system of AI agents that scan only publicly available sources, including community discussions, historical archives, review platforms, and partner materials, and analyze them to estimate likely market pricing ranges and negotiation patterns.

This isn't insider information or leaked data. It's competitive market intelligence derived entirely from public signals, analyzed systematically.

For now, Shadow Pricing Auditor operates as a manual service. I personally review every audit before delivery to ensure accuracy, clarity, and responsible interpretation while the automated platform is being developed.

FAQ

Is this legal?

Yes. All analysis is based exclusively on publicly accessible information such as official pricing pages, public forums, historical web archives, and openly available partner materials. We don't access private data, confidential contracts, or insider information. This service provides competitive market analysis, not verified pricing or privileged knowledge.

How accurate is the pricing data?

We provide estimated pricing ranges, not guaranteed quotes.

High confidence means multiple recent public sources align. Medium or low confidence reflects limited, older, or conflicting signals.

Final pricing always depends on vendor discretion, deal size, and negotiation. We strongly recommend verifying directly with the vendor.

How long does delivery take?

You'll receive a PDF report by email within 24 hours. Every audit is manually reviewed before delivery.

What if you can't find enough pricing data?

If we're unable to gather sufficient public information to produce a meaningful analysis, we'll notify you within 2 hours and issue a full refund.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. If you're not satisfied with the report, reply within 7 days and we'll issue a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you offer subscriptions?

Not at the moment. Shadow Pricing Auditor currently offers one-off audits. If you need ongoing competitive intelligence, contact us to discuss a custom arrangement.

What format is the report?

A professionally formatted PDF, delivered by email. Each report includes:

  • Published list pricing (if publicly available)
  • Estimated market pricing range
  • Confidence assessment
  • Methodology and source types
  • Negotiation considerations and caveats
