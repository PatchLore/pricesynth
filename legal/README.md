---

## Quick start

1. **Review** CHECKLIST-UK.md and RED-FLAG-AUDIT.md.
2. **Insert** TERMS-DRAFT-LIABILITY.md into your full Terms of Service and get solicitor sign-off.
3. **Apply** UI copy from UI-COPY-HOMEPAGE-DASHBOARD-EXPORT.md (homepage, dashboard, exports).
4. **Implement** feature flags and API/export rules in TECHNICAL-SAFEGUARDS.md.
5. **Run** the checklist before go-live and tick sign-off.

---

## Core principle

**We provide inputs (market range, your price). We do not provide outputs (conclusions, recommendations, or "you are overpaying by X").** That keeps the product in data aggregation, not professional services.
